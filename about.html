Personal Story

Environmental Engineering Graduate & Geospatial Specialist.

Overview

I am an Environmental Engineering graduate (B.Tech, Kathmandu University) currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Environmental Engineering at the Technical University of Munich (TUM). My specialization focuses on Environmental Hazards, Risk Management, and Water Resources.

My professional journey bridges the gap between traditional environmental modeling and modern data science. With experience in advanced GIS, Remote Sensing, and Python programming, I build tools to analyze complex hazard scenarios—from flood risk in Bavaria to glacier dynamics in Nepal.

Currently, I apply these skills as an Intern on the Geospatial Solutions Team at Munich Re, developing production-grade software for climate risk assessment.
