Investigated long-term glacier dynamics (1990 to 2020) in the Marshyangdi river basin to assess climate change effects. Involved intensive processing of multi-temporal satellite imagery (Landsat 4/5/8 and Sentinel-2) and application of Machine Learning algorithms to map clean vs debris-covered ice.

Post-Disaster LULC Change Detection

Conducted a study using Remote Sensing satellite imagery to analyze and quantify LULC change patterns in the Panauti and Banepa Valley following the 2015 earthquake. The analysis focused on Change Detection and incorporated essential ground validation techniques.
